# Executive Summary

AQAAR-INTELLIGENCE-LAYER-v2 was generated from AQAAR-KB-ACQ-FINAL-v3.zip only. It creates the production data layer for concierge conversation flows, intent detection, qualification, project recommendations, lead scoring, dashboard metrics, sales handoff summaries, and retrieval quality tests.

The package includes 240 lead scenarios, 1440 runtime events, 1200 audit records, 80 recommendation rules, and 24 retrieval tests. Mawjan and Dusit Thani Residences Ajman are prioritized where their enriched v3 KB records are present.

Unpublished values remain unknown, including contact fields in seed leads and any ROI, yield, or payment terms not present in v3.
